PART ONE (COMPLETE PARTS A-F)

  • In the code there are 6 different teams (A,B,C,D,E and F)
  • Each team must implement their planet class following the instructions in the TODO sections of their respective file (TeamA.js, TeamB.js, etc.)
  • Each planet must be customized and include orbiting moons, Blender models/props/creatures placed on the planet surface, and interactive click events.
  • The end goal is that for each class section the result will be one fully working 3D solar system made up of all planets (A-F) orbiting the sun

INSTRUCTIONS (ALL TEAMS)

  1. Choose a theme for your team's planet.
  2. Create a planet using THREE.SphereGeometry (Radius must be between 1.5 and 2).
  3. Give it a custom material using THREE.MeshStandardMaterial.
  4. Use castShadow and receiveShadow on the mesh and all future ones so they can cast and receive shadows.
  5. Add the planet mesh to the planet group.
  6. The moons should rotate around the planet just like the planet group rotates around the Sun.
  7. Load Blender models to populate the planet with multiple props and critters by adding them to the planet group.
  8. Make sure to rotate the models so they are oriented correctly relative to the surface of the planet.
  9. Use raycasting in the click() method below to detect clicks on the models, and make an animation happen when a model is clicked.
  10. Use your imagination and creativity!
